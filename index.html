I'm a fith-year PhD student at Penn State, advised by prof. Carina Curto.

I like network dynamics, topology and neuroscience.

My PhD research focused in understanding and formalizing, from a mathematical perspective, the relationship between structure and function in a very special family of Threshold-Linear Networks. Broadly, I am interested in how the architecture of a neural network shapes its dynamics. During my PhD, I used these networks to (1) model various neural functions and (2) derive theoretical insights about their dynamics.

I am now on the job market, looking to join an experimental lab.
